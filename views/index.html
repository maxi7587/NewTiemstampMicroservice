Timestamp Microservice

Pass a string with a valid date in the URL (as in the examples) to get both unix and natural timestamps.

Examples:

https://newtimesatamp-microservice.glitch.me/January 1, 2016
https://newtimesatamp-microservice.glitch.me/1-1-2016
https://newtimesatamp-microservice.glitch.me/1451606400 (unix timestamp)
